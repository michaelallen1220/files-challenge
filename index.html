Captain Jean-Luc Picard

Captain Jean-Luc Picard was the captain of the USS Starship Enterprise on the show Star Trek: The Next Generation. The show ran for 7 seasons and filmed four movies. He is one of the more popular captains over the many incarnations of Star Trek. The Borg are one of his greatest enemies. During the season three finale Picard is turned into a Borg before regaining his human self at the start of season four.

Captain Jean-Luc Picard originated from the Star Trek universe.
